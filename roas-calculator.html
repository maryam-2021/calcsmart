What is a ROAS Ad Spend Calculator?

Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) helps you see if your marketing is profitable.

ROAS = Total Revenue / Total Ad Spend

📝 In-Depth Guide: Mastering ROAS Ad Spend Calculator

Most businesses aim for a ROAS of 4x or higher to cover operations and product costs.

📌 Example Calculation

Spend $1,000 on Facebook ads to make $4,000 revenue? That's a 4x ROAS.
