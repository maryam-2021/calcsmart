What is a Budget Calculator?

A simple monthly budget tracker to help you visualize your spending and identify saving opportunities.

Savings = Income − Expenses

📝 In-Depth Guide: Mastering Budget Calculator

Budgeting is about telling your money where to go instead of wondering where it went. A solid budget is the foundation of every financial success story.

The 50/30/20 Rule

A popular budgeting framework: 50% of income goes to Needs (rent, utilities, food), 30% to Wants (hobbies, dining out), and 20% to Savings or Debt Repayment. Use our results to see how close you are to this healthy gold standard.
