xⁿ Exponent Calculator

Calculate powers and exponents of any number.

What is a Exponent Calculator?

The Exponent Calculator performs powers and exponentiation for any base number raised to a positive or negative power.

y = xⁿ

📝 In-Depth Guide: Mastering Exponent Calculator

Exponents (or powers) represent repeated multiplication. In the expression xⁿ, x is the base and n is the exponent. It tells you to multiply the base by itself n times.

Rules of Exponents

  • Product Rule: xᵃ × xᵇ = xᵃ⁺ᵇ
  • Quotient Rule: xᵃ / xᵇ = xᵃ⁻ᵇ
  • Power Rule: (xᵃ)ᵇ = xᵃᵇ
  • Zero Exponent: Any number (except zero) raised to the power of 0 is 1.
  • Negative Exponents: x⁻ⁿ = 1 / xⁿ. They represent the reciprocal of the base raised to the positive power.

Real-World Applications

Exponents are the key to understanding exponential growth. This appears in compound interest calculations, population biology, and the Richter scale for earthquakes. Small changes in the exponent lead to massive changes in the final result.

Example

📌 Example Calculation

2³ = 2 × 2 × 2 = 8. Conversely, 10⁻² = 1 / 100 = 0.01.
