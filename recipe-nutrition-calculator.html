What is a Recipe Nutrition Calculator?

The Recipe Nutrition Calculator allows you to list out the ingredients of any meal or recipe to get a complete breakdown of its macronutrients (calories, carbohydrates, protein, and dietary fat). This uses a reference database of common whole foods and ingredients to provide accurate estimates instantly.

Total Calories = Carbs(4 kcal/g) + Protein(4 kcal/g) + Fat(9 kcal/g)
