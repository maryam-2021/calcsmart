What is a Pace Calculator?

Calculate your running pace per kilometer or mile based on your total time and distance.

Pace = Total Time / Distance

📝 In-Depth Guide: Mastering Pace Calculator

Pace is the "currency" of running. It tells you exactly how fast you need to go to hit your goal time for a 5k, 10k, or a Marathon.

Training with Intent

Using a pace calculator lets you set training zones. "Easy runs" should be significantly slower than your race pace to build endurance without injury, while "Intervals" should be faster to build speed.
