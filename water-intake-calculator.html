What is a Water Intake Calculator?

Determine your optimal daily water intake based on your body weight and activity level.

Base = 0.033L per kg of Body Weight

📝 In-Depth Guide: Mastering Water Intake Calculator

Hydration is the simplest way to improve your health. Every system in your body, from your kidneys to your brain, relies on adequate water to function.

The Signs of Dehydration

Thirst is actually a late sign of dehydration. Other early indicators include daytime fatigue, headaches, and dark-colored urine. By hitting your calculated goal, you can improve your energy levels and skin health.
