📝 In-Depth Guide: Mastering Tip Calculator

Tipping etiquette varies wildly by culture and industry. In the United States, tipping is a standard part of the service economy, especially in restaurants.

The 15-20% Standard

Traditionally, 15% is the baseline for acceptable service, while 20% or more is customary for excellent service. Our calculator helps you find these exact amounts instantly, even when the bill has complicated totals.

Splitting the Bill

Dining with friends is better when the math is fair. Use our "Number of People" field to ensure everyone pays their fair share of both the meal and the gratuity.
