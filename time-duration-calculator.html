What is a Duration Calculator?

Calculate the exact time difference (duration) between two specific dates and times.

Duration = |Date2 − Date1|

📝 In-Depth Guide: Mastering Duration Calculator

Time duration stretches across days, hours, and minutes. This calculator provides a complete breakdown so you can understand the exact length of an event.

Applications

Use this for calculating billable hours, finding the length of a long-haul flight, or determining exactly how much time is left until a significant deadline.
